UIU Rescue Rover Team Recruitment 2025

Are you enthusiastic about Robotics, Space exploration, Research, and the search for life beyond Earth? This is the perfect opportunity for you! We are seeking dedicated individuals who are enthusiastic about contributing to the continuous development of our rover. We are excited to build a new core team for upcoming events such as the University Rover Challenge 2026, Anatolian Rover Challenge 2026, and European Rover Challenge 2026. We welcome applications from students of all departments at UIU.

We are looking for members with the following skills:

Mechanical Sub-Team

3D-Design:

  • SolidWorks
  • Fusion 360
  • Blender3D

Fabrication:

  • Basic mechanical knowledge (materials/instruments) is preferred.
  • Understanding of different materials (metals, plastics, composites) and their properties.
  • Skill in using various measuring instruments and tools.
  • Familiarity with different mechanical tools.

Software & Autonomous Sub-Team

  • Previous experience with projects (not required but a plus).
  • Experience in image processing, specifically object detection and segmentation using ML/DL.
  • Preliminary knowledge of basic processing units and sensors.
  • Technical content and report writing skills.

Electrical Sub-Team

  • Circuit and PCB design.
  • Circuit development.
  • Coding in microcontrollers / microprocessors (Arduino, STM32, ESP32, Raspberry Pi, Platform IO, MicroPython, etc.).
  • Knowledge in basic electronics.
  • Module design.

Aerial & Communication Sub-Team

  • Previous experience with drones (not required but a plus).
  • Knowledge in networking, specifically remote operation, communication protocols, and telemetry.
  • Preliminary knowledge of basic sensors and radio modules.
  • Technical content and report writing skills.

Science Sub-Team

  • Students from Pharmacy, Biotechnology, and Genetic Engineering are encouraged to apply.
  • Interest in astrobiology or exobiology is preferable.
  • Experience in research and documentation.
  • Knowledge in biochemistry and organic chemistry.

Media and Public Relations Sub-Team

  • Students from Media Studies and Journalism departments are encouraged to apply.
  • Content writing capabilities.
  • Content creation skills (cinematography, graphic design, voice artistry, video editing).
  • Strong communication skills.

Logistics

  • BBA students are encouraged to apply.
